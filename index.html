Shalaka Mirgal: From IT Professional to Newborn Photography Enthusiast

Hi, I'm Shalaka Mirgal. I'm a mother to a beautiful 1-year-old daughter and have worked as a professional IT engineer for 10 years. My passion for photography blossomed through my love for capturing moments with my daughter. I am a certified newborn and maternity photographer dedicated to ensuring your baby's safety and comfort while creating unique and heartwarming setups. I specialize in documenting your journey from the excitement of your baby bump to your little one's early precious moments and milestones. Don't miss the chance to freeze those fleeting moments of innocence and cuteness before they grow up—time flies, and they won’t stay tiny forever. Investing in these captured moments and cherishing them later is truly remarkable.
